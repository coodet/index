The Time Line Of Phones

  • 1876: Alexander Graham Bell received the first telephone patent for his device.
  • 1930s: Henry Dreyfuss created the Model 302, which is considered to be the first modern telephone
  • 1960s: Phones got even smaller.
  • 1973: The first mobile phone was invented.
  • 1983: The first commercially available mobile phone was launched.
  • 1992: The first smartphone was invented.
  • 2007: The first iPhone was launched.
